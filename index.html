The Song "Un Pirivin Vali" is my song about the pain of a boy's love and the song concept covers sad emotional lyrics. I have composed drop-beat music for this track. The song is recorded and edited in FL Studio. The mastering process involved the sound adjustment of volume EQ and compressions like adding Fades, Cross Fades, and other final touch modulations with a polished sound as a final output. The song is sung and composed by me.






The Song "Usuraiya Tholachen" is a composition by Stephen Zechariah who is a Tamil Singer. His skills and demonstration inspired me to make an unplugged cover of his song. I recorded the vocals and the base of the guitar and piano from FL Studio for making this song, The whole process is done by myself.






The Song "Kadhal Vaithu" is a composition by Yuvan Shankar Raja who is a Tamil Singer. This particular song resonates with my soul so I made an unplugged cover of his song. I recorded the vocals on a normal recorder of a mobile phone and edited them in Lexis Audio App from mobile for making this song, The whole process is done by myself.






The Song "Kaun Tujhe" is a composition by Amaal Mallik who is a Hindi Singer. The lyrics and musical patterns touched my heart so I made an unplugged cover of his song. I recorded the vocals and the base of the guitar and piano from FL Studio for making this song, The whole process is done by myself.






The Song "Vaseegara Hindi Version" is a song sung by Bombay Jayshree who is a Hindi Female Singer. Her song differs from the Tamil version and has its unique version so I decided to make a cover of her song. I recorded the vocals on FL Studio and the music was downloaded from youtube and edited to a Lofi version. The whole process is done by myself.
